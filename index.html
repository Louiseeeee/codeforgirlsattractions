A moment of stillness in the beating heart of a vibrant city. A space that’s alive with music and prayer, art and history. A place where awe-inspiring architecture lifts the eyes – and raises the spirit. Bath Abbey is many things to many people. But whether you come here to join a service or a tour, light a candle or listen to music, what unites us all is a sense of wonder. There is so much to discover.

The Royal Crescent is a row of 30 terraced houses laid out in a sweeping crescent. Built between 1767 and 1774, it is among the greatest examples of Georgian architecture to be found in the United Kingdom. The 150 m crescent was the first crescent of terraced houses to be built, and has stunning views over the parkland opposite. Come and see the grandeur for yourself.
